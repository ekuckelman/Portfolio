About Me

I have spent the last year of my life transitioning between being a second grade teacher and making my way into the tech field. This transition has not been easy. It has required determination, organization, creativity, and a strong work ethic. I began this wild journey by attending a coding bootcamp in downtown Denver called Turing school of Software and Design. While making my way through Turingâ€™s frontend development program I discovered a great deal about software and myself. I discovered that even though I have a passion for technology, I am more passionate about people and addressing everyday needs of the user. This led me down the path to becoming a UX Designer. I am currently taking classes online for UX Design while searching for a company that I can grow with whose values align with my own.

Through my experience as a teacher I had the opportunity to be responsible for the safety of others, build relationships, perform administrative duties, and support co-workers. Teaching also allowed me the opportunity to handle sensitive matters in a professional and kind way with students, parents, and administration. I take extreme care to pay attention to details, communicate effectively, and handle issues in a professional manner. I may not be a teacher anymore but I will always have the drive and passion of a teacher and the work ethic of a life-long learner.
